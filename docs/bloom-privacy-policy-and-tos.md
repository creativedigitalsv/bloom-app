Bloom Mobile AB ("Bloom", "we", "our") operates the Bloom mobile application designed for pregnant couples in Sweden. We are registered under Swedish law and comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

**Data Controller:** Bloom Mobile AB

## 2. Data We Collect

| Category | Data Points | Purpose |
|----------|------------|---------|
| Account | Name, email, password (hashed) | Authentication |

| Partner | Partner email/name | Shared experience |

## 3. Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)
- **Contract performance** – to deliver the app service
- **Legitimate interest** – app analytics and security
- **Consent** – marketing emails (opt-in only)

---

## 4. Data Retention
- Active accounts: retained for duration of subscription + 12 months
- Deleted accounts: purged within 30 days
- Anonymised analytics: retained up to 3 years

## 5. Your Rights
Under GDPR you have the right to:
- ✅ Access your data
- ✅ Rectify inaccurate data
- ✅ Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
- ✅ Data portability
- ✅ Object to processing
- ✅ Lodge a complaint with **Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY)**

To exercise your rights: **privacy@bloom.se**

## 6. Data Transfers
Data is stored in EU-based Supabase servers (Frankfurt, Germany). No data is transferred outside the EEA without adequate safeguards.

---

## 7. Cookies & Tracking
The mobile app does not use browser cookies. We use anonymous session tokens stored securely on-device.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy
Bloom is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We will notify users via in-app notification and email at least 14 days before material changes take effect.
